Privacy Policy

Demo webshop notice: This website is a university exercise. Checkout and payment are mocked. No real orders are processed and no personal data is stored on a server.

1. Controller

Sockora GmbH
Demo Street 12, 1010 Vienna, Austria
Email: privacy@sockora.example

2. What data we process

  • Cart data (products, size, quantity) stored in your browser via localStorage.
  • Cookie choice (accepted/rejected) stored in your browser via localStorage.
  • Contact form data (name, email, message) when you submit the contact form (mock).

3. Purposes and legal bases

  • Cart functionality: to provide webshop functionality (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Cookie preference storage: to remember your choice (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Contact requests: to respond to your message (Art. 6(1)(b) or (f) GDPR).

4. Cookies and localStorage

This website uses localStorage to store cart items and your cookie choice. No tracking cookies are set. Any analytics tools mentioned here are mocked and not active in this demo.

5. Analytics (mocked)

In a real webshop, analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) may be used to measure website usage. In this demo project, analytics is not active. If it were active, cookies would be used only with your consent.

6. Payment processing (mocked)

This demo webshop does not process real payments. The checkout form is a simulation and clears the cart locally.

7. Data retention

Cart and cookie-choice data remain in your browser until you clear your browser data or the localStorage entries. Contact form entries are not stored on a server in this demo.

8. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and to object. If you believe your data is processed unlawfully, you may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: privacy@sockora.example
