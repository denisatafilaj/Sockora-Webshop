About Sockora

Sockora is a fictional sock brand created for a university exercise. This page documents brand elements and trademark forms according to the assignment requirements.

Our brand meaning

Sockora combines comfort-first essentials with modern patterns. The brand stands for daily quality, minimal design, and consistent visual identity.

Trademark forms (3 types)

1) Word mark

SOCKORA

Used for product branding, website title, and packaging.

2) Figurative mark (logo)

A simple sock icon with the brand name.

SOCKORA
Figurative mark (demo)

3) Pattern mark

A repeating diagonal pattern used on socks and packaging.

Nice classes

  • Class 25: Clothing, including socks.
  • Class 35 (optional): Online retail services (webshop).

Trademark research (demo)

To reduce the risk of trademark infringement, the brand name and visual elements were checked using EUIPO eSearch and TMView for identical or highly similar marks in relevant classes (25 and 35). This project uses fictional branding for educational purposes.
